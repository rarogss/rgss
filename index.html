Raro GeoSpatial Solutions

About Us

Raro GeoSpatial Solutions (RGSS) is an IPA registered business specialized in providing professional services on soft-infrastructure development services such as Geographic Information System (GIS), Remote Sensing, GPS, Community Mapping and Carbon Accounting services in Papua New Guinea. Raro GeoSpatial Solutions owners are originally from Papua New Guinea.


Government bodies, local communities and the private sector now realize the need for spatial information with accurate and most up-to-date maps and database to make optimal spatial planning decisions. It is therefore a goal for RGSS to partner with venturing companies and groups to implement GIS, Remote Sensing, GPS, Community Mapping and Carbon Accounting to achieve the best results from these applications.

What we can do for you:

  • Geographic Information System (GIS)
  • Remote Sensing
  • Global Positioning System (GPS) Surveys
  • Community/Participatory Mapping for Incorporated Land Groups (ILG) Documentation
  • Carbon Accounting
  • Drone Mapping
