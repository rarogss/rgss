Raro GeoSpatial Solutions

About Us

Raro GeoSpatial Solutions (RGSS) is a 100% Papua New Guinea owned and is registered under the PNG Incestment Promtion Auathority. RGSS is a business specialized in providing professional services on soft-infrastructure development services using Geographic Information System (GIS) and Remote Sensing. Other specialty areas include GPS, Community Mapping and Carbon Accounting services in Papua New Guinea. Raro GeoSpatial Solutions owners are originally from Papua New Guinea.


Government bodies, local communities and the private sector now realize the need for spatial information with accurate and most up-to-date maps and database to make optimal spatial planning decisions. It is therefore a goal for RGSS to partner with venturing companies and groups to implement GIS, Remote Sensing, GPS, Community Mapping and Carbon Accounting to achieve the best results from these applications.

What we can do for you:

  • Spatial analysis using ArcGIS of forest management or conservation area
  • Crop site suitability analysis using PNGRIS 2007 in ArcGIS
  • Satellite image mosaicing
  • Satellite image classification
  • Satellite image georeferencing
  • Satellite image enhancement
  • Global Positioning System (GPS) Surveys
  • Community/Participatory Mapping
  • Biomass mapping using raster dataset
  • Drone aerial mapping
  • Drone image processing via Open Source applications
